# Contributing to EcoMoveQuest

We appreciate your interest in contributing to EcoMoveQuest! This document provides some guidelines and best practices for contributing to the project.

## Code of Conduct

By participating in this project, you agree to abide by our [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md). Please ensure that your interactions with other contributors are respectful and inclusive.

## Getting Started

1. **Fork** the repository to create your own copy.
2. **Clone** your fork to your local machine.
3. **Set up** your development environment by installing any necessary dependencies.
4. **Create a branch** for your new features or bug fixes.

## Submitting Changes

1. **Develop** your new features or fixes on your branch.
2. **Test** your changes to ensure they are working correctly and do not introduce new issues.
3. **Commit** your changes with a descriptive commit message.
4. **Push** your changes to your fork.
5. **Create a pull request** to merge your changes into the main repository.
6. **Describe** your changes in the pull request, including any related issues or necessary context for the maintainers.
